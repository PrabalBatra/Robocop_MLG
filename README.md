### 🌐 Revolutionizing Highway Safety Through Autonomous Surveillance

**Robo-Cop** is **India’s First Spherical Surveillance Drone (SSD)** — an intelligent, autonomous aerial monitoring system built to **detect highway accidents in real time**, improve emergency response speed, and ensure uninterrupted surveillance through **smart automation and auto-charging capabilities**.

🚨 **Smarter Monitoring. Faster Rescue. Safer Highways.**

</div>

---

## 🌍 Overview

Road accidents remain one of the major causes of delayed emergency response and loss of lives.

**Robo-Cop** aims to redefine highway surveillance using:

- 🚁 **Autonomous spherical drone surveillance**
- 🎯 **Real-time accident detection**
- 📡 **Smart sensor integration**
- ⚡ **Automated emergency response**
- 🔋 **Self-charging capability**
- 🌐 **Continuous monitoring of highways**

Unlike traditional monitoring systems, Robo-Cop offers **real-time aerial visibility**, enabling faster accident identification and minimizing delays in emergency response.
